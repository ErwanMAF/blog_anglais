Porto Travel – Day 2

📍 Date : 13 May 2025

Summary of the day

We had our first free breakfast at the hotel, then we got our metro tickets and headed to ISLAGAIA University. In the morning, we took a Portuguese language class, and at lunchtime, we grabbed a sandwich at Lidl before continuing with the afternoon agility course.

Afterwards, we went back to the airport to pick up a rental car for the rest of our stay. Once at the airport, we also had a small meal at a nearby restaurant before heading to the car rental counter. Finally, we returned to the hotel to wrap up the day.
